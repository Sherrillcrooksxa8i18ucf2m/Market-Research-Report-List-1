<p><ul><li>Unidirectional BMI Prepreg</li><li>Fabric BMI Prepreg</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bismaleimide (BMI) prepreg is available in two main types: Unidirectional BMI Prepreg and Fabric BMI Prepreg. Unidirectional BMI Prepreg consists of continuous fibers aligned in one direction, providing high strength and stiffness in that direction. Fabric BMI Prepreg, on the other hand, includes woven or stitched fabric reinforcements, offering isotropic properties and easier handling during layup. Both types of BMI prepreg are used in aerospace, automotive, and other high-performance applications for their superior mechanical and thermal properties.</p></p>
