<p><p>The Motorcycle Protective Helmets market consists of four main types: Full Face Helmet, Off-Road Helmet, Open Face Helmet, and Half Helmet. Full Face Helmets provide complete head and face protection, ideal for high-speed riding on roads. Off-Road Helmets are designed for dirt biking and offer enhanced ventilation and visor protection. Open Face Helmets cover the top and sides of the head, leaving the face open. Half Helmets provide minimal coverage and are commonly used for casual urban riding. Each type serves specific purposes tailored to different riding styles and preferences.</p></p>
