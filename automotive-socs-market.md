<p><ul><li>Digital</li><li>Analog</li><li>Mixed Signal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive SoCs are divided into three main market types: Digital, Analog, and Mixed Signal. Digital SoCs predominantly process digital signals, enabling applications such as data processing, communication, and control functions. Analog SoCs focus on processing analog signals, making them suitable for applications like audio amplification and sensing. Mixed Signal SoCs combine both digital and analog functionalities, allowing for advanced features like power management and signal conditioning. Each market type caters to different automotive applications, offering a wide range of solutions to meet the industry's diverse needs.</p></p>
