<p><p>Solar wall lights are available in two main types based on the direction of light emitted: light direction down and light direction upward. Light direction down solar wall lights are designed to illuminate the ground or pathway below, providing functional lighting for safety and visibility. Both types offer aesthetic and practical benefits for outdoor spaces.</p></p>
