<p><p>Fly Ash Microspheres are widely categorized into two types based on their structure: Hollow Fly Ash Microspheres and Solid Fly Ash Microspheres. The Hollow Fly Ash Microspheres are hollow in structure, lightweight, and possess insulating properties, making them ideal for applications in lightweight cement, composites, and insulation materials. On the other hand, Solid Fly Ash Microspheres have a solid structure, making them suitable for use in fillers, polymers, ceramics, and other construction materials where structural reinforcement is required.</p></p>
