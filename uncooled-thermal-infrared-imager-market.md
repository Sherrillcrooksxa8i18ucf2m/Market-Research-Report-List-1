The two main types of technologies used in these imagers are vanadium oxide (VOx) and amorphous silicon (A-Si). VOx imagers are known for their high sensitivity and image quality, making them suitable for military and surveillance applications. On the other hand, A-Si imagers are cost-effective and widely used in commercial applications such as thermography, automotive, and industrial monitoring.</p></p>
