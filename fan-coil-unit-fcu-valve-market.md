<p><p>Two Pipe FCU Valve refers to a type of fan coil unit system where both heating and cooling functions are provided by the same pipes. This allows for a simpler and more cost-effective installation process. On the other hand, Four Pipe FCU Valve systems have separate pipes for heating and cooling, providing greater control and flexibility in maintaining different temperatures in various zones. This system is typically used in buildings where precise temperature control is required, but can be more complex and expensive to install.</p></p>
