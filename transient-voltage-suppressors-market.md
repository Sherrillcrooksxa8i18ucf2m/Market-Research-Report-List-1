Uni-polar TVS are commonly used in applications where only one polarity of voltage spike is expected, while Bi-polar TVS are used in applications where voltage spikes can occur in both directions. Both types of TVS play a crucial role in safeguarding electronic devices from damage caused by voltage transients.</p></p>
