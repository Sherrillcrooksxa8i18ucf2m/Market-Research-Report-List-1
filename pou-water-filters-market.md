<p><p>POU Water Filters are categorized into three main types - Gravity, UV, and RO filters. Gravity filters use gravity to filter water through a cartridge, UV filters use ultraviolet light to kill bacteria and viruses, and RO filters use a semipermeable membrane to remove impurities. Each type of filter serves a specific purpose in providing clean and safe drinking water. These filters are popular in both residential and commercial settings due to their convenience and effectiveness in improving water quality.</p></p>
