<p><p>Set-style yogurt is a type of goat milk yogurt that is thick and creamy due to the use of added stabilizers like gelatin or pectin. This yogurt is typically flavored and sweetened before fermentation. On the other hand, Swiss-style yogurt is another type of goat milk yogurt that is known for its velvety texture and mild flavor. This yogurt is made by straining out whey during the fermentation process, resulting in a thicker, smoother consistency compared to other yogurt types.</p></p>
