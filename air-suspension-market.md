<p><p>Air suspension systems can be classified into two main types: Electronically Controlled Air Suspension System and Non-Electronically Controlled Air Suspension System. </p><p>Electronically Controlled Air Suspension System utilizes sensors and an electronic control unit to adjust air pressure and suspension settings, providing a more precise and automated ride quality. Non-Electronically Controlled Air Suspension System, on the other hand, relies on manual adjustment of air pressure by the driver or operator, offering a simpler and more cost-effective alternative for vehicles that do not require advanced suspension technology.</p></p>
