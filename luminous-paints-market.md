<p><p>Luminous paints are divided into two main types: those containing radioactive substances and those containing light-charged substances. Radioactive substance luminous paints emit light through the decay of radioactive materials, while light-charged substance paints absorb light energy and then release it slowly over time. Each type offers unique benefits and characteristics that cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
