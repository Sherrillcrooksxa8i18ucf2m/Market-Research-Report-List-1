<p><p>Air duct sensors are crucial components in HVAC systems. Temperature sensors monitor and regulate the temperature within a duct, ensuring optimal conditions. Pressure sensors help maintain air pressure levels within the duct to prevent issues such as leakage. Wind sensors can detect airflow direction and velocity for efficient ventilation. Air quality sensors monitor pollutants and particles in the air, ensuring a clean and healthy indoor environment. Together, these sensors work to maintain comfort, efficiency, and safety in HVAC systems.</p></p>
