<p><p>The luxury wax candles market includes two main types, paraffin and synthetic candles. Paraffin candles are made from petroleum byproducts and are known for their longer burn times and strong scent throw. On the other hand, synthetic candles are made from various non-natural materials like gel or soy wax, offering a cleaner burning experience and a wider range of scents. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with paraffin candles being more traditional and synthetic candles appealing to those looking for eco-friendly options.</p></p>
