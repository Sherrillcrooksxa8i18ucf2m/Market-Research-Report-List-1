<p><p>Box making machines are available in two main market types: semi-automatic and fully-automatic. Semi-automatic machines require some manual input from operators, such as loading materials and adjusting settings, while the machine performs the majority of the manufacturing process automatically. Fully-automatic machines, on the other hand, are capable of completing the entire box making process without any manual intervention. Both types of machines cater to different production needs and offer varying levels of efficiency and convenience for manufacturers.</p></p>
