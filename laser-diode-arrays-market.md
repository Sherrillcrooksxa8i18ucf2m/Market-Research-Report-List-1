<p><strong>The Laser Diode Arrays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microchannel Cooled</li><li>Conductively Cooled</li><li>Fluid Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laser Diode Arrays come in three main market types: Microchannel Cooled, Conductively Cooled, and Fluid Cooled. Microchannel Cooled arrays use microchannels to dissipate heat, allowing for high-power operation. Conductively Cooled arrays rely on direct contact with a heat sink to regulate temperature, making them suitable for lower power applications. Fluid Cooled arrays use a liquid coolant to maintain proper operating temperature, ideal for high-power and high-duty cycle applications. Each type offers unique cooling solutions to meet different power and performance requirements.</p></p>
