<p><p>The Anti-skid Chain market can be divided into two main types: Metal Anti-skid Chains and Non-metal Anti-skid Chains. Non-metal Anti-skid Chains, on the other hand, are made of materials like rubber or plastic and are lightweight and easy to install. They are gentler on road surfaces but may not be as durable as metal chains.</p></p>
