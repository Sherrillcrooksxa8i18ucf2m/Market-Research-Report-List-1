<p><p>Front dash cameras and rear dash cameras are two types of automotive driving recorders that help drivers capture footage of their surroundings while driving. Front dash cameras are mounted on the front windshield and record a driver's perspective of the road ahead. Rear dash cameras are mounted on the rear windshield or license plate and record the view behind the vehicle. These devices are used for various purposes such as providing evidence in case of accidents, monitoring driving behavior, and improving overall safety on the roads.</p></p>
