<p><ul><li>Monolayer Titanium Alloy Honeycomb</li><li>Multilayer Titanium Alloy Honeycomb</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Titanium Alloy Honeycomb markets are classified into two types: Monolayer and Multilayer. Monolayer Titanium Alloy Honeycomb consists of a single layer of hexagonal cells, providing high strength and durability. On the other hand, Multilayer Titanium Alloy Honeycomb consists of multiple layers of hexagonal cells stacked on top of each other, offering even greater strength and performance. Both types are widely used in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction for their lightweight yet robust properties.</p></p>
