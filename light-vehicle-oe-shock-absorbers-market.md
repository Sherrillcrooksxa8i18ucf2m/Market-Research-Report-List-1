<p><strong>The Light Vehicle OE Shock Absorbers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Twin-tube Type</li><li>Mono-tube Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Light Vehicle OE Shock Absorbers market includes two main types - Twin-tube Type and Mono-tube Type. Twin-tube shock absorbers consist of two concentric tubes where the outer tube acts as the pressure tube and the inner tube serves as the reserve tube. This type is generally more cost-effective and provides a smooth ride. On the other hand, Mono-tube shock absorbers feature a single tube design that allows for better heat dissipation and overall performance. This type is typically used in high-performance vehicles for improved handling and stability.</p></p>
