<p><p>Wearable exoskeletons come in two main types: full body exoskeletons and part of body exoskeletons. Full body exoskeletons cover the entire body and provide support for various physical activities, such as lifting heavy objects or standing for long periods. Part of body exoskeletons are designed to assist specific parts of the body, such as the arms or legs, and can help with tasks like gripping objects or walking. Both types of exoskeletons aim to enhance human strength and mobility in different ways.</p></p>
