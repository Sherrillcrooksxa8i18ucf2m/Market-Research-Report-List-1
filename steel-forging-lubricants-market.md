<p><strong>The Steel Forging Lubricants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Graphite-based Forging Lubricants</li><li>Non-Graphite-based Forging Lubricants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steel Forging Lubricants are essential in the manufacturing process to reduce friction and wear on metal components. Graphite-based Forging Lubricants contain graphite particles that provide excellent lubrication and anti-seize properties. On the other hand, Non-Graphite-based Forging Lubricants utilize alternative materials such as molybdenum disulfide or ceramics to achieve similar lubricating effects. Both types of lubricants play a crucial role in the steel forging industry by improving the efficiency of the forging process and extending the lifespan of tools and dies.</p></p>
