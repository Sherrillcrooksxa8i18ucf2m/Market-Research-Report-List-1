<p><p>Vehicle Switching and Track Switching are two market types in Personal Rapid Transit systems. In Vehicle Switching systems, passengers switch between vehicles at designated stations to reach their destination. This allows for flexibility in routes and minimizes wait times. In Track Switching systems, individual vehicles are able to switch tracks to reach their destination, providing a more direct and efficient route. Both types offer efficient and convenient transportation options for passengers in urban areas.</p></p>
