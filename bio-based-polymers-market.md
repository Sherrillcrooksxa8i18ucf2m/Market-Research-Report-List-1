<p><p>Bio-based polymers can be categorized into bio-degradable and non-degradable markets. Bio-degradable polymers are designed to break down into natural components over time, reducing waste and environmental impact. On the other hand, non-degradable bio-based polymers maintain their structure and properties for longer periods, making them suitable for durable applications. Both types of bio-based polymers offer sustainable alternatives to traditional petroleum-based plastics, contributing to a more eco-friendly and circular economy.</p></p>
