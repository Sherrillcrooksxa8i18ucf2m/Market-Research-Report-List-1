<p><p>Conductivity meters are devices used to measure the ability of a solution to conduct an electrical current. There are three main types of conductivity meters on the market: portable, in-line, and bench-top. Portable meters are small and lightweight, making them ideal for field measurements. In-line meters are designed to be installed directly in a pipeline or process for continuous monitoring. Bench-top meters are larger and more accurate, usually used in laboratory settings. Each type of meter offers unique advantages depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
