<p><p>The Wood and Furniture Paint Additives market is segmented into two main types: solventborne and waterborne. Solventborne additives are solvent-based, offering durability and resistance to chemicals and weather, but pose environmental and health risks. On the other hand, waterborne additives are water-based, eco-friendly, and low in VOCs, making them a popular choice for consumers looking for safer options.
