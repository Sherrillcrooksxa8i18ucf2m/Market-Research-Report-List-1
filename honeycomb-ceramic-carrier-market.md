<p><strong>The Honeycomb Ceramic Carrier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight-through Carriers</li><li>Wall Flow Carriers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Honeycomb Ceramic Carrier Market can be divided into two main types: Straight-through Carriers and Wall Flow Carriers. Straight-through carriers allow gas to flow directly through the channels, providing high flow rates and low pressure drop. On the other hand, Wall Flow Carriers force the gas to pass through the porous walls, allowing for effective filtration and removal of particulate matter. Both types of carriers are commonly used in catalytic converters and diesel particulate filters for automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
