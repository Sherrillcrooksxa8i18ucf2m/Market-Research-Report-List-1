<p><ul><li>Manual</li><li>Semi-automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The motorcycle clutch market is divided into two primary types: manual and semi-automatic. Manual clutches require the rider to manually engage and disengage the clutch by using the clutch lever. This type of clutch offers full control over gear changes but requires more coordination from the rider. On the other hand, semi-automatic clutches automate the engagement process to some extent, making gear changes smoother and easier for the rider. This type of clutch is ideal for those looking for a balance between manual control and convenience.</p></p>
