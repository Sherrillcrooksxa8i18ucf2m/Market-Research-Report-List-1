<p><p>Spearfishing wetsuits are categorized into two types: one-piece spearfishing wetsuits and two-piece spearfishing wetsuits. One-piece spearfishing wetsuits are designed as a single unit, providing full body coverage and insulation. They are typically made of neoprene material, ensuring warmth and protection against the cold water. On the other hand, two-piece spearfishing wetsuits consist of separate jackets and pants. This allows for customization of the wetsuit's fit and enables divers to mix and match different thicknesses for optimal thermal performance. Both types of wetsuits serve the purpose of keeping spearfishers warm and comfortable underwater.</p></p>
