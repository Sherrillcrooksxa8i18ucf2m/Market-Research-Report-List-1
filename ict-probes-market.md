<p><p>ICT probes are categorized into two main types: Short Stroke Test Probes and Long Stroke Test Probes. Short Stroke Test Probes are designed for applications where precision and accuracy are crucial, such as testing small components or delicate circuits. On the other hand, Long Stroke Test Probes are used for applications where a deeper reach or longer contact is required, such as testing through thick materials or reaching components in awkward positions.
