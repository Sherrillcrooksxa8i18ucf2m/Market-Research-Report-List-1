<p><p>Lemongrass extract is commonly available in two primary forms - oil and hydrosol. Lemongrass oil is extracted from the plant's leaves and is known for its strong and refreshing aroma, commonly used in aromatherapy, personal care products, and food flavoring. On the other hand, lemongrass hydrosol is a byproduct of the steam distillation process used to extract the oil and is co-produced in the process. Hydrosol is milder in scent compared to oil and is often used in skin care products and as a natural room or linen spray.</p></p>
