<p><strong>The Rubber Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tires</li><li>Radial Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber tires market can be categorized into two main types - bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires are constructed with crisscross layers of cord running across the tread at a 30-45 degree angle, providing stiffer sidewalls and better puncture resistance. They are commonly used in off-road vehicles and heavy-duty trucks. On the other hand, radial tires have cord layers that run at a 90-degree angle to the direction of travel, allowing for better flexibility and heat dissipation. They are widely used in passenger vehicles and commercial trucks.</p></p>
