<p><p>RFID equipment market can be categorized into four types based on frequency range: LF RFID Reader, HF RFID Reader, UHF RFID Reader, and MW RFID Reader. LF RFID Readers operate at low frequencies suitable for short-range communication. HF Readers work at higher frequencies and are commonly used in access control systems. UHF Readers operate at ultra-high frequencies, allowing for longer range communication. MW Readers work at microwave frequencies for specialized applications. Each type of RFID reader serves different purposes based on its frequency range and communication range.</p></p>
