<p><p>Additive Manufacturing (3D Printing) market types include Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM), both of which utilize a high-powered laser or electron beam to melt and fuse metallic powders layer by layer to create intricate metal parts. Another market type includes technologies such as Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) and Stereolithography (SLA), which use thermoplastic materials to build 3D objects. Each of these market types offers unique advantages in terms of material compatibility, accuracy, and speed in the additive manufacturing process.</p></p>
