<p><p>Carbon Fiber Reinforced Polymers (CFRP) are composite materials made of carbon fibers embedded in a polymer matrix. These materials are known for their high strength, stiffness, and lightweight properties, making them widely used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction. Two main types of CFRP are thermosetting CFRP, where the polymer matrix is set by heat or chemical reaction, and thermoplastic CFRP, where the polymer matrix can be re-melted and reshaped. Each type has specific applications and advantages in different sectors of the market.</p></p>
