<p><p>Refractory mud is a specialized material used in high-temperature applications such as furnace linings and kilns. There are four main types of refractory mud on the market: clay refractory mud, high alumina refractory mud, silicon refractory mud, and magnesia refractory mud. Each type offers unique properties that make them suitable for different industrial processes. Clay refractory mud is versatile and cost-effective, while high alumina mud offers excellent resistance to heat and corrosion. Silicon mud is known for its high thermal conductivity, and magnesia mud is valued for its high refractoriness and strength.</p></p>
