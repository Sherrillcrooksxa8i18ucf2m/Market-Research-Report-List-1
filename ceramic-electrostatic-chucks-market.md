<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ceramic Electrostatic Chucks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ceramic Electrostatic Chucks are classified into two main types: Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks. Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks work by inducing an electric charge on the chuck to attract and hold the wafer in place. On the other hand, Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks use a combination of Coulomb forces and electrostatic forces to hold the wafer securely. Both types of chucks are widely used in semiconductor manufacturing processes for their ability to securely hold wafers during processing.</p></p>
