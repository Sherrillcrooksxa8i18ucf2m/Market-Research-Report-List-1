<p><p>The Energy Monitoring and Control System (EMCS) market consists of two main types: Compact Type and Combination Type. On the other hand, Combination Type EMCS involve a combination of separate monitoring and control modules, offering greater flexibility and scalability for larger and more complex energy management systems. Both types cater to different needs and requirements in the market.</p></p>
