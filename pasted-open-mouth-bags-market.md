<p><p>Pasted open mouth bags are commonly available in two main market types: single bag and multi-layer bag. Single bags are made from a single layer of material and are generally more cost-effective and suitable for packaging products with less sensitive storage requirements. On the other hand, multi-layer bags consist of two or more layers of material, offering enhanced protection for products that require barrier properties such as moisture resistance, oxygen barrier, or prolonged shelf life. The choice between single and multi-layer bags depends on the specific packaging needs of the product.</p></p>
