<p><p>Sound-absorbing underlays are classified into various types based on their material composition. Synthetic underlays, such as polyethylene, polyurethane, and rubber, offer durability and moisture resistance. Plant-based underlays are eco-friendly options that provide good sound absorption properties. Mineral underlays offer excellent acoustic performance and resistance to compression. Felt underlays are known for their softness and ability to reduce impact noise. Asphalt underlays provide thermal insulation in addition to sound absorption.
