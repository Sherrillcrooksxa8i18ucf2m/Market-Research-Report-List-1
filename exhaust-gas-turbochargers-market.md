<p><strong>The Exhaust Gas Turbochargers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial flow</li><li>Axial flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exhaust gas turbochargers can be classified into two main types: radial flow and axial flow. Radial flow turbochargers have a centrifugal compressor and are known for their compact size and high efficiency. They are commonly used in gasoline engines. On the other hand, axial flow turbochargers have an axial compressor and are favored for their suitability in diesel engines. They provide a higher mass flow rate compared to radial flow turbochargers. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the engine.</p></p>
