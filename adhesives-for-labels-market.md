<p><p>Adhesives for labels market can be categorized into two main types: Permanent Adhesive and Removable Adhesive. Permanent Adhesives are designed to create a strong and lasting bond between the label and the surface, ensuring that the label stays in place for a long period of time. On the other hand, Removable Adhesives are specifically formulated to allow easy removal of the label without leaving a residue or causing damage to the surface. These two types cater to different needs and preferences of consumers in various industries.</p></p>
