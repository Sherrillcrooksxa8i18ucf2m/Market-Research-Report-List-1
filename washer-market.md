<p><p>Washers are essential components in various industries, with different types serving specific purposes. Plain washers are flat, smooth rings that distribute the load of a threaded fastener, while spring washers provide flexibility and prevent loosening due to vibrations. Locking washers have teeth or ridges that grip the fastener and prevent rotation. There are also other types of washers available in the market, such as square washers and fender washers, each designed for specific applications and requirements in construction, automotive, and manufacturing industries.</p></p>
