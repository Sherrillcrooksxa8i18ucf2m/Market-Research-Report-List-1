<p><strong>The Quartz Kitchen Countertops Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quartz kitchen countertops market types include press molding and casting molding. Press molding involves compressing a mixture of quartz and resin under high pressure to create a solid slab with consistent color and pattern. On the other hand, casting molding involves pouring the quartz and resin mixture into a mold to set and cure into a desired shape. Both methods result in durable and stylish quartz countertops that are resistant to stains, scratches, and heat.</p></p>
