<p><strong>The Super Resolution Microscope System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>STED</li><li>SSIM/SIM</li><li>STORM</li><li>FPALM</li><li>PALM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Super Resolution Microscope Systems utilize advanced imaging techniques to achieve resolutions beyond the diffraction limit of traditional microscopes. Different types include STED (Stimulated Emission Depletion), SSIM/SIM (Structured Illumination Microscopy), STORM (Stochastic Optical Reconstruction Microscopy), FPALM (Fluorescence Photoactivation Localization Microscopy), and PALM (Photoactivated Localization Microscopy). STED uses a doughnut-shaped depletion beam to achieve super resolution, SSIM/SIM utilizes patterned illumination, STORM and FPALM use stochastic switching of fluorescent molecules, and PALM relies on the precise localization of individual molecules to achieve high resolutions.</p></p>
