The market for Satellite AIS is divided into two main types: Class A Transponders and Class B Transponders. Class A Transponders are used by larger vessels and are required to transmit data at regular intervals, while Class B Transponders are used by smaller vessels and are not required to do so. Both types of transponders play a crucial role in enhancing maritime safety and security.</p></p>
