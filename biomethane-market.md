Energy crops, such as corn or grass, are used to produce biomethane. Agricultural waste, including crop residues, is another source. Sewage sludge-derived biomethane is obtained from wastewater treatment plants. Finally, industrial food processing waste is converted into biomethane. These different sources contribute to the diverse biomethane market.</p></p>
