<p><p>Fluorescent acrylic sheets are available in two main types: cast acrylic sheet and extruded acrylic sheet. Cast acrylic sheets are manufactured by pouring liquid acrylic resin into a mold and allowing it to harden, resulting in a durable and high-quality product. On the other hand, extruded acrylic sheets are produced by pushing acrylic resin through a die, creating a less expensive option with good impact resistance. Both types of acrylic sheets offer vibrant fluorescent colors that are perfect for signage, displays, and other applications.</p></p>
