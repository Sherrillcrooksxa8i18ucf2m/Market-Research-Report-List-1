<p><p>Infrared detection equipment market consists of various types of technologies such as Mercury cadmium telluride (MCT), Indium gallium arsenide (InGaAs), Pyroelectric, Thermopile, and Microbolometer. MCT offers high sensitivity and fast response time. InGaAs is suitable for near-infrared applications. Pyroelectric sensors detect infrared radiation through changes in temperature. Thermopile sensors use multiple thermocouples to measure infrared radiation. Microbolometers offer high resolution and are commonly used in thermal imaging cameras. These technologies cater to different applications and industries within the infrared detection market.</p></p>
