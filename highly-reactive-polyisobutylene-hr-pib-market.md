<p><p>Highly Reactive Polyisobutylene (HR-PIB) is categorized into three main types based on molecular weight: Up to 1,000 g/mol, 1,001–1,500 g/mol, and above 1,500 g/mol. These different market types cater to specific applications where the molecular weight of the polymer is crucial. Lower molecular weight HR-PIB (up to 1,000 g/mol) is used for applications requiring faster curing times, while higher molecular weight HR-PIB (above 1,500 g/mol) is used for applications where increased durability and resistance to chemical degradation are important.</p></p>
