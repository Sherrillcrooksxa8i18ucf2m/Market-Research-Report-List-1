<p><p>Organic protein powders can be categorized into two main types based on their source: animal source proteins and vegetable source proteins. Vegetal source proteins, on the other hand, are obtained from plant-based sources like pea, soy, or rice, making them suitable for vegans and individuals with dietary restrictions. Both types offer a convenient and effective way to increase protein intake and support muscle growth and recovery.</p></p>
