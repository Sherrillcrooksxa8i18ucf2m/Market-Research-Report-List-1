<p><strong>The Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Very Low EVA</li><li>Low EVA</li><li>Mid EVA</li><li>High EVA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ethylene Vinyl Acetate (EVA) resin market is segmented into four types based on the vinyl acetate content: Very Low EVA, Low EVA, Mid EVA, and High EVA. Very Low EVA resin typically contains less than 4% vinyl acetate and is used for high strength applications. Low EVA resin has 4-14% vinyl acetate content and is suitable for medium strength applications. Mid EVA resin with 14-25% vinyl acetate content is used for flexibility and impact resistance. High EVA resin contains over 25% vinyl acetate and is used for softness and elasticity.</p></p>
