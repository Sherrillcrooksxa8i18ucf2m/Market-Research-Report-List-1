<p><p>Viscose carbon fiber is classified into three main types based on fiber length: continuous, long, and short. Continuous viscose carbon fibers are uninterrupted strands used in high-performance applications. Long fibers are commonly used in automotive and aerospace industries for their superior strength and durability. Short fibers are typically mixed with other materials in composites for improved mechanical properties. Each type of viscose carbon fiber offers unique benefits and is suitable for various applications depending on the specific requirements of the end product.</p></p>
