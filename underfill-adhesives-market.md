<p><strong>The Underfill Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Component Underfill Adhesives</li><li>Two-Component Underfill Adhesives</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underfill adhesives are typically categorized into two types: Single Component Underfill Adhesives and Two-Component Underfill Adhesives. Single Component Underfill Adhesives are pre-mixed and require no additional components, making them convenient and easy to use. On the other hand, Two-Component Underfill Adhesives consist of separate resin and hardener components that must be mixed together before application. This type allows for greater control over the curing process and is often chosen for its superior performance in demanding applications.</p></p>
