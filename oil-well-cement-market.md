<p><ul><li>Ordinary</li><li>Moderate Sulfate-Resistant</li><li>High Sulfate-Resistant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oil-Well Cement can be classified into three main types based on their sulfate resistance levels- Ordinary, Moderate Sulfate-Resistant, and High Sulfate-Resistant. Ordinary oil-well cement is suitable for wells with low sulfate concentrations, while Moderate Sulfate-Resistant cement is designed for moderate sulfate environments. High Sulfate-Resistant cement is specifically formulated for wells with high levels of sulfate, providing superior resistance to sulfates. Each type of cement serves a specific purpose in ensuring the integrity and longevity of oil wells in various environmental conditions.</p></p>
