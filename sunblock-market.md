<p><p>Physical Sunblock works by sitting on top of the skin and reflecting UV rays away, while Chemical Sunblock absorbs UV rays before they can penetrate the skin. Physical Sunblock tends to be thicker and leave a white cast, while Chemical Sunblock is typically easier to apply and more cosmetically elegant. Both types offer protection against harmful UV radiation, but individuals may have personal preferences based on their skin type and specific needs. Ultimately, choosing the right sunblock is a matter of personal preference and skin sensitivity.</p></p>
