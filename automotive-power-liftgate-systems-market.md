<p><p>The automotive power liftgate systems market consists of three main types - Spindle Unit, ECU (Control Unit), and Touch Sensor (Pinch Sensor). The Spindle Unit is responsible for controlling the opening and closing mechanism of the liftgate. The ECU serves as the brain of the system, managing various functions and inputs. The Touch Sensor, or Pinch Sensor, detects obstacles and ensures safe operation of the liftgate. Overall, these components work together to provide convenient and safe operation of power liftgate systems in vehicles.</p></p>
