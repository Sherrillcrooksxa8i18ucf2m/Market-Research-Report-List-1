<p><p>Low temperature grease is classified into three main types based on the base oil used: mineral base oil, synthetic base oil, and biological base oil. Mineral base oil greases are derived from petroleum and offer good lubrication properties at low temperatures. Synthetic base oil greases are made from chemically engineered molecules, providing enhanced performance and stability in cold conditions. Biological base oil greases are derived from renewable sources, making them environmentally friendly options for low temperature applications.</p></p>
