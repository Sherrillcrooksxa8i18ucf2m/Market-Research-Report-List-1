<p><p>Pure Tequila is made from 100% blue agave and is considered to be of higher quality compared to Mixto Tequila, which is made from a combination of at least 51% blue agave and other sugars. Pure Tequila is known for its smooth taste and authentic agave flavor, appealing to connoisseurs and those looking for a more refined drinking experience. Mixto Tequila, on the other hand, is more affordable and commonly used in cocktails due to its lighter taste and cheaper production costs. Both markets cater to different consumer preferences and budget constraints.</p></p>
