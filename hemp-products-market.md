<p><p>Hemp products market can be categorized into two main types based on fiber length: long (bast) fibers and short (core) fibers. Long fibers are extracted from the outer layer of the hemp plant stalk and are traditionally used in textiles, ropes, and paper. On the other hand, short fibers come from the inner core of the hemp stalk and are commonly used for industrial purposes such as insulation, animal bedding, and biofuels. Both types of fibers have unique properties that make them valuable in different industries.</p></p>
