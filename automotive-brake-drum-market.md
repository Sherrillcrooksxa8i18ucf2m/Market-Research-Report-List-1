<p><p>An automotive brake drum is a crucial component of the braking system found in vehicles. It is a cylindrical-shaped cast iron or aluminum drum attached to the wheels. The increasing production and sales of vehicles, especially passenger cars and commercial vehicles, are driving the demand for brake drums. Additionally, the growing awareness about vehicle safety and the implementation of stringent safety regulations by various governments and regulatory bodies are further propelling market growth.</p><p>In recent years, there have been advancements in brake drum technologies to improve their performance and durability.
