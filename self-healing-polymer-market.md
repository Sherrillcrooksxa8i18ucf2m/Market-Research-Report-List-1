<p><p>Self-healing polymers are innovative materials designed to repair damage automatically without external intervention. They are classified into three main types: vascular, intrinsic, and capsule-based. Vascular self-healing polymers have a network of channels that deliver healing agents to the damaged area. Intrinsic self-healing polymers contain reversible bonds that enable them to heal when damaged. Capsule-based self-healing polymers have microcapsules filled with healing agents that rupture upon damage, releasing the mending material. Each type offers unique advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
