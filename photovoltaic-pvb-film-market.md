<p><p>Photovoltaic PVB film is available in two main market types: transparent and opaque. Transparent PVB film allows light to pass through, making it ideal for applications where aesthetics and visibility are important while still providing solar energy generation. Opaque PVB film, on the other hand, blocks light transmission and is more suitable for applications where privacy or shading is desired. Both types of PVB film offer solutions for integrating solar energy generation into various building materials and design concepts.</p></p>
