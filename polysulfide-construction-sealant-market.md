<p><ul><li>Moisture Cured Polysulfide Construction Sealant</li><li>Heat Cured Polysulfide Construction Sealant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polysulfide Construction Sealant is available in two main types based on the curing process: Moisture Cured and Heat Cured. Moisture Cured sealants cure when they come in contact with moisture in the air, making them suitable for outdoor applications. Heat Cured sealants, on the other hand, require high temperatures to cure properly. Both types offer durable and flexible sealing properties for construction applications, but the curing process is the key difference between them.</p></p>
