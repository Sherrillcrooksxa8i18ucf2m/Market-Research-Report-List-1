<p><p>Decoration Terrazzo Flooring market consists of two main types: Inorganic Terrazzo and Epoxy Terrazzo. Inorganic Terrazzo is made from materials like marble, glass, and quartz mixed with a binder. It is durable and resistant to stains and scratches. Epoxy Terrazzo is a more customizable option, made from epoxy resin mixed with colorful aggregates. It offers a wide range of design possibilities and is known for its seamless finish. Both types of terrazzo flooring are popular choices for commercial and residential spaces due to their beauty and longevity.</p></p>
