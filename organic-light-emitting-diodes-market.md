<p><ul><li>Hole Transport Layer Materials (HTL)</li><li>Electron Transport Layer Materials (ETL)</li><li>Electrode materials</li><li>Emissive Layer Materials (EML)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Organic Light Emitting Diodes (OLED) market is categorized based on different types of materials used in the manufacturing process. This includes Hole Transport Layer Materials (HTL), Electron Transport Layer Materials (ETL), Electrode materials, and Emissive Layer Materials (EML). HTL materials facilitate the movement of positively charged holes, while ETL materials aid in the movement of negatively charged electrons. Electrode materials help in the flow of current, and EML materials are responsible for emitting light when a current passes through them.</p></p>
