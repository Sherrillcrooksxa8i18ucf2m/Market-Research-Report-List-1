<p><p>Non-woven materials market can be classified into two major types: durables and disposables. Durables refer to non-woven products that are designed for long-term use, such as upholstery, geotextiles, and automotive components. Disposables, on the other hand, are single-use non-woven products like medical gowns, wipes, and face masks. Durables cater to industries requiring high durability and longevity, while disposables cater to industries needing convenience and cost effectiveness. Both types play crucial roles in various sectors, reflecting the versatility of non-woven materials.</p></p>
