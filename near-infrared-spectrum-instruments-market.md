<p><strong>The Near Infrared Spectrum Instruments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Near Infrared (NIR) Spectrum Instruments are used in various industries for analysis and measurement of materials. They can be categorized into three types: Filter Type, Dispersion Type, and Fourier Transform Type. </p><p>Filter Type instruments transmit specific wavelengths of light through filters to measure the absorbance of the material. Dispersion Type instruments split the NIR light into different wavelengths using a prism or grating. Fourier Transform Type instruments use an interferometer to measure the interference caused by the sample's absorption of light at different wavelengths. Each type has its own advantages and applications based on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
