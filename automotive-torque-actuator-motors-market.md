<p><p>Automotive torque actuator motors come in three main types: pneumatic, electric, and mechanical. Pneumatic actuators are powered by compressed air and are commonly used for high-speed applications. Electric actuators are powered by electricity and are known for their precise control and quiet operation. Mechanical actuators use mechanical components such as gears and levers to convert energy into rotational motion. Each type has its own advantages and is chosen based on factors such as speed requirements, precision, and cost.</p></p>
