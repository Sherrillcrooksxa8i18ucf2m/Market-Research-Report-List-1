<p><p>Multilayer organic diplexers market can be classified into crystal type, ceramics type, and others. Crystal type diplexers are made using crystal materials such as quartz or lithium niobate, providing high frequency stability and low insertion loss. Ceramics type diplexers are made using ceramic materials like alumina or titania, offering good thermal resistance and durability. Other types may include diplexers made from composite materials or hybrid designs. Each type offers unique advantages based on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
