<p><ul><li>Fixed</li><li>Telescopic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Offshore gangways are used to provide safe passage for personnel between a vessel and an offshore platform. There are two main types of gangways: fixed gangways and telescopic gangways. Fixed gangways are permanently installed structures that provide a stable walkway from the vessel to the platform. Telescopic gangways, on the other hand, are retractable and can be adjusted in length to accommodate varying distances between the vessel and the platform. Both types of gangways play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of personnel during offshore operations.</p></p>
