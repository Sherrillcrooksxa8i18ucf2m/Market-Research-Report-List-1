<p><p>Torque vectoring system is a technology that enhances vehicle handling by managing power distribution to individual wheels, allowing for better traction, stability, and cornering performance. This system actively adjusts the torque sent to each wheel based on factors like vehicle speed, steering angle, and road conditions, thereby improving overall driving dynamics.</p><p>The torque vectoring system market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.6% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for better driving dynamics and safety features in vehicles is a major factor contributing to the growth of this market. Moreover, the rising adoption of electric and hybrid vehicles, which often come equipped with torque vectoring systems for improved efficiency and performance, is also driving market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the torque vectoring system market is the integration of this technology with other advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS) like electronic stability control and adaptive cruise control. This integration allows for more seamless and efficient vehicle control, further enhancing driving performance and safety. Additionally, advancements in sensor technology and software algorithms are enabling more sophisticated torque vectoring systems that can adapt to changing driving conditions in real time.</p></p>
