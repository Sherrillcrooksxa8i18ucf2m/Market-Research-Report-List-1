<p><p>Wool auxiliary market types include Cross Linking Agents, Emulsifiers, Fixing Agents, Dispersing Agents, Defoamers, and others. Cross Linking Agents are used to improve wool fiber strength and durability. Emulsifiers help in the blending of different materials during processing. Fixing Agents help in setting dyes on wool fibers. Dispersing Agents aid in even distribution of dyes on fibers. Defoamers reduce foam formation during processing. Other auxiliaries may include softeners, anti-static agents, and UV absorbers for additional functionalities.</p></p>
