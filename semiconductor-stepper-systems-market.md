<p><p>The Semiconductor Stepper Systems Market consists of two main types - Stepper Motor Systems and Drives Systems. Stepper Motors Systems involve the use of specialized motors that divide a full rotation into a series of individual steps. This allows for precise control of movement in semiconductor manufacturing equipment. Drives Systems, on the other hand, provide the necessary power and control signals to operate stepper motors effectively. Both these systems play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and efficient semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
