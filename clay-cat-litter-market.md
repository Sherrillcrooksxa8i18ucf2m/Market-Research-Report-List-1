<p><p>Clay cat litter is available in two main market types: small grain and big grain. Small grain clay litter is finely granulated, allowing for better liquid absorption and odor control. It is preferred for its ability to clump easily, making cleaning the litter box more convenient. On the other hand, big grain clay litter consists of larger granules that provide better airflow and less tracking. It is ideal for cats who prefer a softer texture to dig in. Both options offer effective odor control and moisture absorption.</p></p>
