<p><p>Reefer semi-trailers are designed to transport temperature-sensitive goods. They are mainly classified into two types based on their functionality: single temperature and multi-temperature. Single temperature reefer semi-trailers are equipped to maintain a consistent temperature throughout the entire trailer for transporting goods that require the same temperature setting. On the other hand, multi-temperature reefer semi-trailers have multiple compartments with different temperature settings, allowing for the transportation of goods that require varying temperature conditions within the same trailer.</p></p>
