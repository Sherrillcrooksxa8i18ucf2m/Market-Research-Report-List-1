<p><strong>The Passenger Count System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infrared Type</li><li>Time-of-Flight Type</li><li>Stereoscopic Vision Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passenger count system market encompasses three main types: Infrared Type, Time-of-Flight Type, and Stereoscopic Vision Type. Infrared Type systems utilize infrared sensors to detect the presence of passengers. Time-of-Flight Type systems measure the time taken for light or sound to travel to objects and back to calculate passenger count. Stereoscopic Vision Type systems use two cameras to detect and count passengers based on depth perception. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in various industries such as transportation, retail, and hospitality.</p></p>
