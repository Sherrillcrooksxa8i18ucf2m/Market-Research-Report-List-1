<p><p>Water-borne protective coatings market includes various types such as epoxy, polyurethane, acrylic, alkyd, polyester, and others. Epoxy coatings offer excellent adhesion, chemical resistance, and durability. Polyurethane coatings provide superior abrasion resistance and UV protection. Acrylic coatings are known for their weather resistance and gloss retention. Alkyd coatings offer fast drying and good color retention. Polyester coatings are valued for their flexibility and corrosion resistance. Other types include silicone, fluoropolymer, and polyvinylidene fluoride coatings, each with unique properties to suit different applications.</p></p>
