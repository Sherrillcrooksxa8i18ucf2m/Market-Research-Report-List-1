<p><ul><li>Hardwired</li><li>Configurable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive power distribution modules come in two main types: hardwired and configurable. Hardwired modules have pre-defined circuit configurations that cannot be easily changed, making them simpler and more cost-effective. On the other hand, configurable modules allow for more flexibility in the allocation of power distribution, as they can be reconfigured based on specific needs. This versatility comes at a higher cost but provides greater customization options for automotive manufacturers. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences within the automotive industry.</p></p>
