<p><p>The Industrial Medium-Voltage Cables Market consists of three main types: XLPE Cables, MI Cables, and EPR Cables. XLPE Cables are known for their high insulation properties and durability. MI Cables, or mineral insulated cables, are known for their resistance to high temperatures and physical damage. EPR Cables, or ethylene propylene rubber cables, are known for their flexibility and resistance to chemicals and environmental factors. Each type of cable caters to different industrial needs and applications in the medium-voltage range.</p></p>
