<p><p>Magnetic float level sensors are available in two main market types: continuous type and liquid level switch type. Continuous type sensors provide real-time monitoring of liquid levels, offering precise and accurate measurement throughout the entire range. On the other hand, liquid level switch type sensors are used for simple on/off control, activating when the liquid reaches a specific predetermined level. Both types of sensors are commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and pharmaceuticals for monitoring and controlling liquid levels in tanks and vessels.</p></p>
