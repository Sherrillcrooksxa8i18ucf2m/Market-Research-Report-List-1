<p><ul><li>WB BGA</li><li>WB CSP</li><li>FC CSP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The BT Substrate market includes three main types: WB BGA, WB CSP, and FC CSP. WB BGA (wire bonding ball grid array) substrates are commonly used in advanced packaging applications. WB CSP (chip scale package) substrates are compact and suitable for smaller electronic devices. FC CSP (flip chip chip scale package) substrates offer high performance and reliability for high-density applications. Each type caters to different market needs and requirements, providing diverse options for manufacturers in the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
