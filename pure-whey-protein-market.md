<p><strong>The Pure Whey Protein Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Whey Protein Concentrate</li><li>Whey Protein Isolate</li><li>Whey Protein Hydrostate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pure whey protein is available in three main types on the market: whey protein concentrate, whey protein isolate, and whey protein hydrolysate. Whey protein concentrate is the most common form, containing around 70-80% protein along with some fat and lactose. Whey protein isolate undergoes further processing to remove more fat and lactose, resulting in a protein content of around 90-95%. Whey protein hydrolysate is pre-digested, making it easier for the body to absorb. Each type offers varying levels of protein purity and can cater to different dietary needs and preferences.</p></p>
