<p><p>Automatic License Plate Reader market types include Fixed Type and Portable Type systems. Fixed Type ALPR systems are permanently installed in specific locations such as traffic signals or toll booths to continuously monitor passing vehicles. Portable Type ALPR systems are movable and can be easily deployed in different locations for temporary use, such as at events or crime scenes. Both types of ALPR systems offer the same functionality of capturing and processing license plate information for various applications such as law enforcement and parking management.</p></p>
