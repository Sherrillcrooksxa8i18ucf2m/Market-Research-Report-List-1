<p><p>Bottom Anti Reflection Coatings (BARC) are used in the semiconductor industry to reduce reflections on the bottom surface of a substrate during lithography processes. There are two main types of BARC coatings: organic and inorganic. Organic BARC coatings are typically made of carbon-based materials, offering good compatibility with photoresists. Inorganic BARC coatings, on the other hand, are composed of metal oxides or nitrides, providing excellent durability and etch resistance. Both types of BARC coatings have their own advantages and are used in various applications within the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
