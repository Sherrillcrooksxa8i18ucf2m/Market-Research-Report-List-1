Wired Digital I/O Modules require physical connections via cables for data transmission, offering reliable and secure communication. On the other hand, Wireless Digital I/O Modules enable communication without the need for physical connections, providing flexibility and convenience in installations where running cables is not feasible. Both types serve different applications based on the specific requirements of the user.</p></p>
