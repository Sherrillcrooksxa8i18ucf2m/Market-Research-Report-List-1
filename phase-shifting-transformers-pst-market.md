<p><strong>The Phase Shifting Transformers (PST) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct (One Core) PST</li><li>Indirect (Two Cores) PST</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Phase Shifting Transformers (PST) are used to control power flow in electrical grids by adjusting the phase angle between different transmission lines. There are two main types of PSTs: Direct (One Core) PST and Indirect (Two Cores) PST. </p><p>Direct PSTs use a single core to achieve phase shifting, resulting in a more compact design and lower cost. Indirect PSTs, on the other hand, use two separate cores which can provide higher power ratings and better performance in certain applications. Both types play a critical role in maintaining grid stability and efficiency.</p></p>
