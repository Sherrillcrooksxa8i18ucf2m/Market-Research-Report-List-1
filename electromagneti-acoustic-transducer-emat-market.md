<p><ul><li>Lorentz Force</li><li>Magnetostriction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electromagnetic Acoustic Transducer (EMAT) market is divided into Lorentz Force and Magnetostriction types. Lorentz Force EMATs utilize a magnetic field to induce Lorentz forces in a material, generating ultrasonic waves for non-destructive testing. Magnetostriction EMATs use magnetostrictive material to transmit and receive ultrasonic waves without the need for a coupling medium. These technologies offer advantages in various industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace due to their non-contact and non-destructive testing capabilities.</p></p>
