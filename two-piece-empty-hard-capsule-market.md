<p><p>The two-piece empty hard capsule market consists of three main types: gelatin, starch & pullulan, and hydroxypropyl methylcellulose (HPMC). Gelatin capsules are made from animal collagen and are the most commonly used type, offering excellent stability and compatibility with a wide range of formulations. Starch & pullulan capsules are plant-based and provide a suitable option for vegetarians. HPMC capsules are made from cellulose and offer high moisture content and chemical resistance, making them suitable for sensitive formulations.</p></p>
