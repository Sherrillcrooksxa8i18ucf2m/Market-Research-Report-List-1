<p><p>Paint and coatings market types include solvent-based coatings, waterborne coatings, high-solids coatings, powder coatings, and other specialty coatings. Solvent-based coatings contain organic solvents that evaporate during the drying process. Waterborne coatings use water as a solvent, making them more environmentally friendly. High-solids coatings have a higher concentration of solids, reducing solvent use. Powder coatings are applied as dry powder and then cured with heat. Other specialty coatings include UV-curable coatings, low-VOC coatings, and epoxy coatings. Each type offers unique benefits for various applications.</p></p>
