<p><p>Software Over The Air (SOTA) updates can be delivered through both cellular and Wi-Fi networks. Cellular market involves updates being sent through mobile networks, providing a wide coverage area but may incur additional data charges. Wi-Fi market involves updates being sent through wireless internet connections, allowing for faster and more cost-effective delivery within a limited range. Both markets cater to different needs, with cellular being suitable for remote or on-the-go updates, and Wi-Fi being ideal for faster and more affordable updates within a specific location.</p></p>
