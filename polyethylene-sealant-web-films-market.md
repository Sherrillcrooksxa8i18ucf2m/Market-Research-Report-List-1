<p><p>Polyethylene sealant web films are commonly categorized into two types: LDPE sealant web films and LLDPE sealant web films. LDPE sealant web films are made from low-density polyethylene and are known for their high clarity and sealability. On the other hand, LLDPE sealant web films are made from linear low-density polyethylene, offering superior puncture resistance and tensile strength. Both types of sealant web films are widely used in various industries for packaging applications due to their excellent sealing properties and durability.</p></p>
