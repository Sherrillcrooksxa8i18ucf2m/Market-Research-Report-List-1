<p><p>Closed molding carbon fiber market includes two main types: chopped carbon fiber and continuous carbon fiber. Chopped carbon fiber is typically used in short fiber reinforced applications such as molding compounds, while continuous carbon fiber is used for longer and higher strength applications like aerospace and automotive components. Chopped carbon fiber offers cost-effective solutions for various industries, while continuous carbon fiber provides superior mechanical properties and is preferred in high-performance applications that require strength and durability.</p></p>
