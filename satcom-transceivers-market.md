<p><p>The SATCOM Transceivers market is divided into different frequency bands, including C Band, Ka Band, Ku Band, L Band, and X Band. C Band transceivers operate in the 4 to 8 GHz frequency range and are commonly used for satellite communications. Ka Band operates in the 26.5 to 40 GHz range, offering higher data rates and capacity. Ku Band transceivers operate in the 12 to 18 GHz range, providing reliable service for data and video transmissions. L Band and X Band transceivers are also utilized for specific satellite communication needs.</p></p>
