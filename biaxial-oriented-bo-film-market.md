<p><p>Biaxially Oriented (BO) Films refer to films that have undergone stretching in both machine and transverse directions during the manufacturing process. There are three main types of BO films - BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene), BoPET (Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate), and BOPA (Biaxially Oriented Polyamide). BOPP films are widely used in packaging, BoPET films are commonly used for flexible packaging and electrical insulation, while BOPA films are known for their high barrier properties and are commonly used in food packaging applications.</p></p>
