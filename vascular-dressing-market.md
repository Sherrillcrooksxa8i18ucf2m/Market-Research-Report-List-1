<p><p>Vascular dressings are categorized into two main types: antimicrobial and non-antimicrobial. Antimicrobial vascular dressings contain agents that help prevent infections and reduce the risk of complications during wound healing. Non-antimicrobial dressings, on the other hand, do not contain these agents and are primarily used to cover and protect wounds without actively combating infections. Both types of vascular dressings play a crucial role in providing effective wound care and promoting healing in patients with vascular conditions.</p></p>
