<p><p>Precision op amps are operational amplifiers that are designed to have very high levels of accuracy and low levels of error in their operation. They provide low offset voltage, low offset drift, high common-mode rejection, and low noise, resulting in high performance and accuracy.</p><p>The Precision Op Amps Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.40% during the forecast period.
