<p><p>The fire cabinet market offers different types of cabinets based on their mounting options. Surface mount cabinets are installed directly onto the wall surface, making them easily accessible in emergency situations. Semi-recessed cabinets are partially installed into the wall, providing a more streamlined appearance while still offering quick access. Fully recessed cabinets are completely embedded into the wall, offering a flush and seamless look. Each type of cabinet has its own advantages and suitability depending on the location and aesthetic requirements.</p></p>
