<p><p>The aircraft materials market includes various types such as aluminum alloys, steel alloys, titanium alloys, super alloys, and composites. Aluminum alloys are lightweight and corrosion-resistant, ideal for structural components. Steel alloys are strong and durable, commonly used in landing gear and engine parts. Titanium alloys offer high strength-to-weight ratio and resistance to high temperatures. Super alloys have excellent creep and oxidation resistance, suitable for turbine engines. Composites combine different materials to achieve desired properties such as strength, stiffness, and reduced weight in aircraft structures.</p></p>
