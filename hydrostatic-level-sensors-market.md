<p><strong>The Hydrostatic Level Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Switch Type</li><li>Continuous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Switch type hydrostatic level sensors are used to detect specific levels of liquid in a tank or container, triggering an on/off signal once that level is reached. Continuous type hydrostatic level sensors provide a real-time, continuous measurement of the liquid level in a tank or container. Switch type sensors are more commonly used in applications where discrete level detection is required, while continuous type sensors are more suited for applications where a precise and continuous level measurement is necessary.</p></p>
