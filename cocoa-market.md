<p><ul><li>Cocoa Liquor</li><li>Cocoa Butter</li><li>Cocoa Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cocoa market can be divided into three main types: Cocoa Liquor, Cocoa Butter, and Cocoa Powder. Cocoa Liquor is the purest form of chocolate, made from grinding roasted cocoa beans. Cocoa Butter is the fat extracted from cocoa beans, commonly used in skincare products and chocolate production. Cocoa Powder is made by removing the cocoa butter from the cocoa liquor, resulting in a fine powder used in baking, beverages, and confectionery. Each of these products plays a vital role in the chocolate industry and cater to different consumer needs.</p></p>
