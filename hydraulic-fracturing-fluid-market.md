<p><p>Hydraulic fracturing fluid market can be categorized into two main types: water-based and oil-based fracturing fluid. Water-based fracturing fluid is made mainly of water, proppants and chemicals. This type is commonly used due to its cost-effectiveness and environmental friendliness. On the other hand, oil-based fracturing fluid is made using oil as the base fluid, along with chemicals and proppants. This type is preferred for its ability to withstand high pressure and high temperatures, making it suitable for challenging reservoir conditions.</p></p>
