<p><p>The Engine Oil Cooler market is segmented into different types based on their cooling mechanism. Air to Oil Coolers (ATOC) use the engine's airflow to cool the oil, while Air to Fluid Coolers (ATF) use a separate cooling fluid. Internal Tube Oil Coolers (ITOC) are integrated within the engine block. Other types may include liquid cooled oil coolers or combination coolers. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of cooling efficiency and performance, catering to a wide range of engine oil cooling requirements.</p></p>
