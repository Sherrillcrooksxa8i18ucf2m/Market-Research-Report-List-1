<p><ul><li>Horizontal Twin-shaft Mixer</li><li>Vertical Twin-shaft Mixer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horizontal Twin-shaft Mixer: This type of mixer has two horizontal shafts that rotate in opposite directions, ensuring thorough mixing of materials. It is widely used in large-scale construction projects due to its high efficiency and consistent mixing performance.</p><p>Vertical Twin-shaft Mixer: In contrast to the horizontal mixer, the vertical twin-shaft mixer has two vertical shafts that rotate in opposite directions. This design is more suitable for smaller batch sizes and is often used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and chemical manufacturing.</p></p>
