<p><p>Spintronics Technology includes Metal Based Devices and Semiconductor Devices markets. Metal Based Devices manipulate the spin of electrons in metallic materials to create spin currents for applications in memory, sensors, and logic devices. Semiconductor Devices utilize the properties of semiconductors to control the flow of spin-polarized electrons for efficient data storage and processing. Both markets are driving innovation in the field of spintronics, with Metal Based Devices offering simplicity and versatility, while Semiconductor Devices provide high performance and integration capabilities.</p></p>
