<p><p>Surface-mounted downlights are installed directly onto the ceiling surface, providing a simple and versatile lighting solution. Recessed downlights, on the other hand, are set into the ceiling, creating a more seamless and streamlined appearance. Both types offer different aesthetic and functional benefits depending on the design and layout of the space. Surface-mounted downlights are easier to install and maintain, while recessed downlights can create a more modern and integrated look in the room.
