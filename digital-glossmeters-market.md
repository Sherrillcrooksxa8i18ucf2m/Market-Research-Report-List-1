<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Digital Glossmeters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Three-Angle Glossmeters</li><li>Dual-Angle Glossmeters</li><li>Single-Angle Glossmeters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Three-angle glossmeters measure gloss at three different angles (20°, 60°, and 85°) to provide a more comprehensive analysis of a surface's glossiness. Dual-angle glossmeters typically measure gloss at two different angles (20° and 60°), offering a balance between detailed analysis and usability. Single-angle glossmeters measure gloss at a fixed angle, usually 60°, providing a quick and simple way to assess surface shine. Each type of glossmeter caters to different industry needs and preferences for gloss measurement.</p></p>
