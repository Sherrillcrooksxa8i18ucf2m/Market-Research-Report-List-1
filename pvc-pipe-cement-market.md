<p><p>PVC Pipe Cement is available in three main types: Regular Bodied, Medium Bodied, and Heavy Bodied. Regular Bodied PVC Pipe Cement is suitable for general plumbing applications while Medium Bodied PVC Pipe Cement is used for drainage systems and irrigation. Heavy Bodied PVC Pipe Cement is designed for bonding larger diameter pipes and fittings. Each type offers varying levels of viscosity and bonding strength, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
