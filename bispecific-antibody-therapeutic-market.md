<p><ul><li>Bearing An Fc Region</li><li>Lacking An Fc Region</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bispecific antibody therapeutics with an Fc region are designed to engage with immune cells, enhancing the immune response against targeted cells, leading to increased efficacy and potentially longer half-life. On the other hand, bispecific antibodies lacking an Fc region are mainly intended for targeting specific cells or molecules without the need for immune cell engagement, offering a more precise and potentially safer therapeutic option. Both types of bispecific antibodies have shown promise in the treatment of various diseases, catering to different therapeutic needs.</p></p>
