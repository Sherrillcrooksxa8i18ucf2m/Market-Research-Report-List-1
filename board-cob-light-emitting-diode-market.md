<p><p>Board (COB) Light Emitting Diode Market includes two main types - Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) and Inorganic Light Emitting Diode (ILED). OLEDs are made of organic compounds that emit light when an electric current is applied, offering high color quality and flexibility. ILEDs, on the other hand, are made of inorganic materials like gallium nitride, providing higher efficiency and brightness compared to OLEDs. Both types are commonly used in various lighting applications due to their energy efficiency and long lifespan.</p></p>
