<p><p>The aerospace landing gear market consists of two main types: main landing gear and nose/tail landing gear. The main landing gear is responsible for supporting the majority of the aircraft's weight during takeoff, landing, and ground operations. It is usually located beneath the aircraft's fuselage and can have multiple wheels. On the other hand, the nose/tail landing gear is found at the front or rear of the aircraft and is responsible for supporting a smaller portion of the aircraft's weight. It typically consists of a single wheel or set of wheels. Both types of landing gear play crucial roles in ensuring safe and efficient aircraft operations.</p></p>
