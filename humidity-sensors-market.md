<p><strong>The Humidity Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Humidity Sensor</li><li>Absolute Humidity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Humidity sensors are classified into two main types: Relative Humidity Sensor and Absolute Humidity Sensor. Relative humidity sensors measure the amount of moisture in the air relative to the maximum possible amount at a given temperature, while absolute humidity sensors directly measure the actual amount of water vapor present in the air. Both types of sensors are used in various applications such as weather monitoring, HVAC systems, and industrial processes to ensure optimal conditions and prevent damage caused by excessive or insufficient moisture levels.</p></p>
