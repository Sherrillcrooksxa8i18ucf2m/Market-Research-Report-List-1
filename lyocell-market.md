<p><ul><li>Normal Lyocell Fiber</li><li>Crosslinked Lyocell Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Lyocell market can be classified into two types, normal Lyocell fiber and crosslinked Lyocell fiber. Normal Lyocell fiber is produced using a solvent spinning process, resulting in a soft and breathable fabric that is widely used in the textile industry for clothing and home textiles. On the other hand, crosslinked Lyocell fiber undergoes a chemical treatment to improve its strength and durability, making it suitable for applications where higher performance is required, such as in automotive and industrial textiles.</p></p>
