<p><p>Advanced Composite Materials (ACM) are materials made up of two or more constituent materials with significantly different physical or chemical properties. The types of ACM include Carbon Fiber, Glass Fiber, Aramid Fiber, Metal Matrix, and Ceramic Matrix. Carbon Fiber composites are known for their high strength-to-weight ratio, Glass Fiber composites offer good stiffness and corrosion resistance, Aramid Fiber composites provide excellent impact resistance, Metal Matrix composites offer high thermal conductivity, and Ceramic Matrix composites offer high temperature resistance and wear properties.</p></p>
