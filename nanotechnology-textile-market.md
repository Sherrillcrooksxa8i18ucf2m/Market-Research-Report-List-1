<p><p>Nanocoated textiles involve the application of nanomaterials on the surface of fabrics to enhance properties like water resistance and UV protection. Nanoporous textiles have nano-sized pores that allow for better breathability and moisture-wicking. Fabrics consisting of nanofiber webs are created using nanotechnology to produce ultra-thin fibers with improved strength and durability. Composite fibers based on nanostructures involve combining different materials at the nanoscale to create textiles with unique properties like lightweight, strength, and conductivity.</p></p>
