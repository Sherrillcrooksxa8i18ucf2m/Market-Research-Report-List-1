<p><p>Fire clay market types are classified based on their properties and uses. Flint fire clay contains a high percentage of silica, making it very resistant to heat and perfect for making refractory materials. Semi-flint fire clay has a lower silica content and is used for more moderate temperature applications. Semi-plastic fire clay has a higher plasticity, making it easier to shape and mold. Plastic fire clay is the most plastic and is used for sculpting and forming intricate shapes. Each type caters to different industrial needs.</p></p>
