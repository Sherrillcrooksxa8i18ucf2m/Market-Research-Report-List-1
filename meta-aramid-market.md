<p><p>Meta aramid market is segmented into two major types: raw white fiber and dope dyed fiber. Raw white fiber is the natural color of aramid fibers and is used in applications where color is not a significant factor. Dope dyed fiber, on the other hand, is dyed during the manufacturing process, resulting in colored fibers that do not require further dyeing. Both types of fibers are widely used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and protective clothing for their high strength and heat resistance properties.</p></p>
