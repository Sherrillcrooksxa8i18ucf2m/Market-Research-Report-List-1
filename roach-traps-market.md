<p><p>Roach traps come in three main market types: bait stations, granular baits, and gel baits. Bait stations are discreet containers that attract roaches with poison or food. Granular baits are small pellets or powders that can be sprinkled in areas where roaches are likely to be present. Gel baits consist of a sticky substance that attracts roaches and contains poison. Each market type offers a different approach to effectively lure and eliminate roaches, catering to various preferences and needs of consumers.</p></p>
