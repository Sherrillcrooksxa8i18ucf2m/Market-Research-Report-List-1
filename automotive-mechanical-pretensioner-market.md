<p><strong>The Automotive Mechanical Pretensioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Buckle Pretensioner</li><li>Retractor Pretensioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Mechanical Pretensioner market can be divided into two main types - Buckle Pretensioner and Retractor Pretensioner. Buckle Pretensioner is designed to tighten the seat belt around the occupant in the event of a collision, ensuring a secure fit and minimizing movement. Retractor Pretensioner, on the other hand, works by using a mechanism to tighten the seat belt within the retractor assembly. Both types of pretensioners play a crucial role in enhancing safety during automobile accidents by reducing the risk of injury to occupants.</p></p>
