<p><p>Coil Coating Additives Market Types include Solventborne and Waterborne additives. Solventborne additives are dissolved in solvents and offer high performance and durability, but raise environmental concerns due to VOC emissions. On the other hand, Waterborne additives are eco-friendly alternatives that use water as a base, reducing VOC emissions and improving sustainability. Waterborne additives are gaining popularity due to increasing environmental regulations and a growing emphasis on sustainability in the coil coating industry.</p></p>
