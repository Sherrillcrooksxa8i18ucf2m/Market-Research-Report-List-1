<p><p>X-ray detector cards are used in security screening to detect concealed items. There are three main types of X-ray detector cards: dual-energy, single-energy, and multi-energy. </p><p>Dual-energy detector cards use two different X-ray energies to help distinguish between materials based on their atomic number and density. Single-energy detector cards use only one X-ray energy source. </p><p>Multi-energy detector cards use multiple X-ray energies to provide more detailed and higher resolution images, making them more effective in detecting a wider range of materials in security screening applications.</p></p>
