<p><strong>The Galvanized Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electro-galvanizing Wire</li><li>Hot-dip galvanized Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Galvanized wire is categorized into two main types: electro-galvanizing wire and hot-dip galvanized wire. Electro-galvanizing wire is a process where a protective zinc coating is applied to steel wire through electrolysis. This type of galvanized wire is known for its smooth and uniform coating. On the other hand, hot-dip galvanized wire involves immersing the steel wire in molten zinc, resulting in a thicker and more durable coating. Both types of galvanized wire are widely used in various industries for their corrosion resistance properties.</p></p>
