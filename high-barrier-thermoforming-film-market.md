<p><p>High Barrier Thermoforming Films are categorized into two main types - Rigid and Flexible. Rigid High Barrier Thermoforming Films are designed to provide a sturdy and protective packaging solution for products that require strong barriers against external elements. On the other hand, Flexible High Barrier Thermoforming Films offer more versatility and are suitable for products that need to be vacuum-sealed or tightly wrapped. Both types of films play a crucial role in extending the shelf-life and maintaining the freshness of various food and non-food products.</p></p>
