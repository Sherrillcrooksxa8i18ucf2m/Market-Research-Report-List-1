<p><strong>The Backup Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Backup camera market can be classified into two main types: CCD and CMOS cameras. CCD cameras use a charge-coupled device to convert light into electronic signals, allowing for high-quality images with low noise levels. On the other hand, CMOS cameras utilize complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, which offers lower power consumption and faster image capture. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, but overall they provide reliable solutions for enhancing safety and convenience while reversing or parking vehicles.</p></p>
