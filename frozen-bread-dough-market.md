<p><ul><li>Fermented</li><li>Unfermented</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Frozen bread dough is available in two main market types: fermented and unfermented. Fermented dough has undergone a process where yeast causes the dough to rise, giving it a light and airy texture. This type of dough is commonly used for products like artisan bread and pizza crusts. On the other hand, unfermented dough has not undergone the rising process, making it denser and suitable for products like dinner rolls and biscuit dough. Both types offer convenience and versatility for consumers looking to bake fresh bread at home.</p></p>
