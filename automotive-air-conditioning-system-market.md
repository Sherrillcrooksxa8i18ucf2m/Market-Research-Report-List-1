<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Air-conditioning System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCOT (Cycling Clutch Orifice Tube) System</li><li>TXV (Thermal Expansion Valve) System</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive air-conditioning system market is segmented into three main types: CCOT (Cycling Clutch Orifice Tube) System, TXV (Thermal Expansion Valve) System, and other systems. The CCOT system regulates refrigerant flow by cycling the compressor on and off, while the TXV system uses a thermal expansion valve to control the refrigerant flow more precisely. Other systems in the market may include electronic expansion valves or variable displacement compressors. Each type offers different benefits and features to cater to the varying needs of automotive air-conditioning systems.</p></p>
