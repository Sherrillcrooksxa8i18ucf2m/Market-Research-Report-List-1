<p><p>Zirconia bioceramic market comprises two main types: biological inert ceramics and bioactive ceramics. Biological inert ceramics are stable, non-reactive materials that do not undergo any chemical reactions with the tissues in the body. On the other hand, bioactive ceramics have the ability to form a strong bond with surrounding tissues and promote bone growth. These materials are designed to actively interact with the biological environment, aiding in the regeneration and repair of damaged tissues.</p></p>
