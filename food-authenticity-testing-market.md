<p><p>Food authenticity testing market is segmented based on different testing methods including PCR, LC-MS, isotope methods, immunoassay, and others. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) is a technique for detecting DNA to identify the origin of food products. LC-MS (Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry) is used for analyzing complex food samples. Isotope methods analyze the stable isotopes in food to determine its authenticity. Immunoassay involves using antibodies to detect specific food components. Other methods include spectroscopy, chromatography, and nuclear magnetic resonance.</p></p>
