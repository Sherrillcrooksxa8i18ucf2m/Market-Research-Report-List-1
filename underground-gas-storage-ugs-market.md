<p><p>Underground Gas Storage (UGS) Market includes three main types: Depleted Fields, Aquifers, and Salt Caverns. Depleted Fields involve storing natural gas in former oil or gas reservoirs. Aquifers involve utilizing porous rock formations to store gas. Salt Caverns involve creating caverns in salt formations to store gas. Each market type has its own advantages and considerations, such as cost, capacity, and location. Companies must carefully assess these factors to determine the most suitable type of UGS for their storage needs.</p></p>
