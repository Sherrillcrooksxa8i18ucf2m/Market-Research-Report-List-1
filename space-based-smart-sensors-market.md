<p><p>Space-based smart sensors are essential components in satellite systems, with two main market types being radiation-hardened and radiation-tolerant. Radiation-hardened sensors are designed to withstand high levels of radiation in space environments without degradation, ensuring reliable performance over long durations. On the other hand, radiation-tolerant sensors are able to continue functioning even when exposed to lower levels of radiation, although they may experience some performance degradation. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in enabling successful space missions and are key considerations for satellite manufacturers.</p></p>
