<p><p>Potassium feldspars are commonly divided into three main market types based on their intended uses: Glass Grade, Ceramic Body Grade, and Glaze Grade. Glass Grade potassium feldspar is used in the manufacturing of glass products due to its high alumina content. Ceramic Body Grade potassium feldspar is ideal for making ceramic products such as tiles and sanitary ware. Glaze Grade potassium feldspar is suited for producing glazes and enamels for ceramics. Each market type has specific characteristics that make them suitable for their respective applications.</p></p>
