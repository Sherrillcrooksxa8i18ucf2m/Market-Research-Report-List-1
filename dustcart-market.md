<p><p>The dustcart market can be classified into three main types: front loaders, rear loaders, and side loaders. Front loaders are designed to be loaded from the front of the vehicle, making them ideal for tight spaces. Rear loaders are loaded from the back of the vehicle, making them efficient for residential waste collection. Side loaders are loaded from the side of the vehicle, allowing for easier access to bins in narrow alleys. Each type of dustcart has its own advantages and is suited for different waste collection needs.</p></p>
