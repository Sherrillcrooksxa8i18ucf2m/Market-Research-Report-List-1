<p><p>Acrylic Thermoplastic Elastomers are categorized into two main types based on their monomer composition: Methyl acrylate and Ethyl acrylate. Methyl acrylate-based TPEs are known for their high flexibility, impact resistance, and weatherability, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. On the other hand, Ethyl acrylate-based TPEs are preferred for their excellent heat resistance and durability. Both types of acrylic thermoplastic elastomers offer unique properties that cater to different industry needs and applications.</p></p>
